Amanda Busick Biography

Amanda Dianne Busick is a famous American sports reporter who works as the National Hot Rod Association’s (NHRA) multi-media reporter on behalf of Fox Sports. Her career began interning at her local news television station in North Carolina and later in Chicago.

Busick’s first on-camera was as a sideline reporter for Time Warner Cable Sports at the North Carolina High School Athletic Association in 2014. She started working for the NHRA in 2016 and was the Formula E pit lane reporter at the New York City ePrix in 2018 and 2019.
