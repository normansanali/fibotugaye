Madison Beer celebrates her brother Ryder's 21st birthday in Las Vegas

LAS VEGAS, NEVADA: Singing sensation, Madison Beer and her brother Ryder Beer celebrated his 21st birthday on Thursday, May 4, at the Zouk Nightclub in Las Vegas, kicking off the festive weekend stay at Resorts World Las Vegas.

Ryder is a music producer, and the siblings routinely post images on social media to show their closeness. Madison posted the sibling duo's throwback photographs on Instagram to wish her brother on his birthday.

Ryder beer's birthday bash

Madison and Ryder danced the night away to music by Louis The Child at the award-winning club, posing for photos with cut-outs of the birthday guy's face only suited for a 21st birthday bash. The evening was capped off with a massive display of Ryder and a birthday message on the Resorts World billboard for all to see on the Las Vegas Strip. Zouk Nightclub was the ideal venue for the event. The unique Vegas scene is located at the intersection of traditional dance space and modern club experience, allowing partygoers to enjoy a variety of feelings while there.

Zouk's innovative approach to the nightclub industry has already been recognised, with the club being selected Best Nightclub by Best of Las Vegas 2022 and Best Resident DJ by Modern Luxury. A Twitter user posted the birthday bash video saying, "Madison Beer with family and friends at Ryder’s birthday party last night, via Zouk Group’s Instagram story."

'You are the most important thing'

Madison made sure to wish her younger brother on social media, posting flashback photographs of the two with a loving remark. "You are the most important thing. i am so lucky to call you my brother. i am so lucky to watch you grow. the funniest and best boy. happy birthday kid. i love you more than you’ll ever know " she wrote for her brother.

Their mom commented, "I love this so much. Nothing better than your kids being besties. A Mom’s greatest gift! That hawk has been around for decades. He now has the Betty boop sweatshirt! Happy Birthday @ryderbeer… can’t wait to celebrate you all weekend."
