Who is Derek Jeter?

Derek Jeter whose birth name is Derek Sanderson Jeter was born on 26 June 1974 in New Jersey in the United States of America. Jeter who would later become the face of the New York Yankees, used to play basketball as well as baseball in his childhood and if his words are to be believed, basketball was his favorite game, not baseball. After finishing high school With the help of a scholarship, he enrolled at the University of Michigan. He was noticed by the world while playing for Michigan State, and people began to consider him a future Major League player. He began his Major League career in 1995.
