George Foster Net Worth

George Foster was born on December 1, 1948 in Tuscaloosa, AL. He was a feared right-handed hitter and a valuable part of the Cincinnati Reds' 'Big Red Machine', helping them to win two World Series titles in 1975 and 1976. He was an integral part of the team's success and is remembered as one of the greatest players in the history of the franchise.
