Is Balm Chicky Balm Balm Still In Business?

In the 1970s, when every teen carried a lip balm and a comb in their bag or back pocket, Balm Chicky Balm Balm was founded as a line of flavorful lip balm. Balm Chicky was created because of its product design. Each Balm Chicky tube had two sides, making it simple to share with a friend without spreading germs.

Lip balm came in a variety of flavors, including Hot Chocolate Love, Huge Cucumber & Mint, Wild Mountain Honey, and Juicy Melons. In February 2015, Balm Chicky Balm Balm’s two founders, Elizabeth Moskow and Abby Schwalb, made a Shark Tank pitch for their company. They got a deal on Shark Tank, but Kevin O’Leary didn’t support them at all in their pitch.

Is Balm Chicky Balm Balm Still In Business?

Currently, Balm Chicky Balm Balm is not operating. The business only lasted three years before its website stopped working in 2018.

Co-founder Moskow is currently the Culinary Director at Sterling-Rice Group, a marketing firm in Boulder, Colorado, according to LinkedIn. In Portland, Oregon, Abby Schwalb works as an independent special event contractor.
