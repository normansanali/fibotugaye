The Career of Wayne Routledge

Wayne Routledge, a well-respected soccer player, had an illustrious career that spanned over 20 years, including 10 years with Swansea City. He began his career in London with Crystal Palace as a youth before making senior appearances with the first team. He then moved to Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur for two years but spent part of that time on loan to Portsmouth and Fulham. He had subsequent stints with Aston Villa, Cardiff City, Queens Park Rangers, Newcastle United, and finally, Swansea City, where he remained until his retirement in 2021.
