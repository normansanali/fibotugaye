#FireJericho Explodes on Social Media As Chris Jericho Allegations About Kylie Rae Surface

Chris Jericho’s talent and contributions to AEW are undeniable at this point. His name blew up on social media on the morning before Worlds End, but it wasn’t for anything that he ever wanted to surface.

Nick Hausman raised serious allegations against Chris Jericho, suggesting hidden issues in his past. Subsequently, another tweet alleged that Kylie Rae had been approached by Chris Jericho in a hotel room, resulting in her departure from AEW.

Following the revelation about Chris Jericho, numerous fans took to social media to express their opinions. Kylie Rae’s name also gained significant attention, with fans rallying in her support.

An old clip of Chris Jericho dismissing Vince McMahon’s hush money allegations also surfaced, and that wasn’t a good look. Now, fans are calling for Jericho to be fired.

#FireJericho is now trending on social media. Tony Khan might want to address this, before it gets out of hand. At this point, this story is taking on a life of its own, as fans are connecting their own dots all over the place.

We will have to see what happens next in this situation. This is a very delicate subject matter, and it must be taken seriously. AEW fans are speaking out, because they may not way to see Chris Jericho anymore.

Chris Jericho is in an 8-man tag team match at AEW Worlds End. He will team with Sting, Darby Allin and Sammy Guevara against Konosuke Takeshita, Powerhouse Hobbs, Ricky Starks, and Big Bill. Only time will tell how live fans react to seeing Jericho in Long Island.
