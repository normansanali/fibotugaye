Matt LeBlanc's Embarrassing Moment at Taylor Swift Concert

Not even Taylor Swift can get Matt LeBlanc’s daughter to think her dad is cool. The Friends star has a 12-year-old daughter, Marina, and thought that would score the ultimate “cool dad” points by taking her to a Taylor Swift concert and even getting up on stage with the pop star.

“Taylor was nice enough to let us come backstage,” he told Conan O’Brien on Tuesday night. “I went backstage and Chris Rock was there with his two little girls. So Taylor met all the girls and then she said, ‘Hey, you know, in one of my videos, the guy from my video is here and he’s the number one male model in the world. I want to introduce him on stage, and then I’d love it if you and Chris came up on stage, and I could introduce you guys as the number two and number three male models in the world.’”

“I had no idea it was coming. Chris and I looked at each other and he hadn’t shaved, you know, not really ready to go on stage at the Staples Center,” he joked, but said he felt obligated to help out Swift. Plus, maybe his daughter would think it was cool.

“We’re like, ‘Whatever you need, Taylor, we’re here to help.’ So next thing I know, we’re underneath this stage,” LeBlanc says. “The model guy, he goes up first and then walks down this long catwalk. And Chris Rock and I are there and I remember just standing there looking at him underneath the stage and thinking, ‘Wow this is very bizarre. I’ve never worked with him before, don’t really know him that well.”

“We both look at each other and just kind of go, ‘Okay, just turn it up to 11 and act like a fool. Here we go. And it was kind of fun, just walk down the stage and dance.”
