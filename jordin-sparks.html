Jordin Sparks aka Jordin Sparks-Thomas is an American singer, songwriter, actress and businesswoman. As of 2023, Jordin Sparks’s net worth is $8 million. She accumulated her net worth through her singing and songwriting career.

However, she is also an established and prominent actress and has proven her worth in that sector and earns a sizable amount of money. Besides she earns a good amount of money from her brand endorsements, commercial appearances, and social media promotions.

Jordin earned a primary breakthrough by winning the 6th season of the hit American tv reality show, American Idol. She was only aged 17 when she won the program making her the youngest in the history of American Idol to do so. Sparks has since established her singing career at an outstanding level.

She has so far released 4 studio albums, 25 Singles, 4 extended plays, 17 music videos, 1 mixtape, and 14 soundtrack appearances. Her music style is Pop and R&B. She has been associated with various music labels including RED, RCA, Legacy, 19, Louder Than Life, etc.
