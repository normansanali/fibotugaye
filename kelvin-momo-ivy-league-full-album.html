Kelvin Momo Ivy League (Full Album)

Multi-talented and versatile South African act, Thato Kelvin Ledwaba, better known by his stage name as Kelvin Momo unveils a new installment named; “Ivy League” album.

The latest star-studded is a great addition to the South African Music Industry. ‘Ivy League’ happens to be Kelvin’s third official studio album after his previous albums; “Bayeke’ and ‘Momo’s Private School’ which were both released in 2020.

These albums were well received among his fans and music lovers. The third album, ‘IvyLeague’ embeds 21 beautifully crafted tunes with fearures from Babalwa M, Sekiwe, Ch’cco, Yumbs, Ta Skipper, Tlholo, Mhaw Keys, TribeSoul, TBO, Yumbs, JaySax, Steve, Mgiftos, Aymos, Sino Msolo, Dalsh, Cooper SA, Babalwa M, Murumba Pitch, Mogomotsi Chosen, S.O.N, Mick Man, TNK Musiq and Killer Punch.

ALSO: Jay Rox – Enigma (Full Album)

Kelvin Momo has been in the music scene for a while and has been consistent throughout the years. Over these years, the self-proclaimed Soulful King has been creating production mixes and churning out songs which has earned him a reputation as a great producer.

‘Ivy League’ is a dope project you will want to add to your playlist and play endlessly. Download Full Album below.
