Is Charles Siebert Alive?

Charles Sieber is no more alive. Charles passed away on May 1 2022 at the age of 84 in Santa Rosa, California, U.S.

Charles Alan Siebert was an actor and television director from the United States most known for his portrayal of Dr Stanley Riverside II on the television series Trapper John, M.D., which he played from 1979 until 1986.

Siebert studied acting at Marquette University under the great Fr. John J. Walsh, S.J., and then at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA).
