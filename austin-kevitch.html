Who is Camila Cabello’s New Boyfriend, Austin Kevitch?

Austin Kevitch (born on July 5, 1991) is 31 years old businessman from Pennsylvania who rose to fame in 2022 after it got confirmed that he is dating Cuban-American singer Camila Cabello. “Cinderella” actress was spotted strolling with Austin in June 2022, after which the rumors about them dating spread like wildfire.

Later in August, a paparazzi caught Camila kissing Kevitch’s cheek. After Cabello’s breakup with Canadian singer Shaun Mendes, it is good to see that she is trying to move on and has found a suitable boyfriend.

Kevitch is the founder of a dating platform named Lox Club, which is exclusively for people with high standards. Unlike other apps, for Lox Club, one has to apply, and only those approved get to be part of the club. Sources say that club currently has more than 20,000 pending applications.

What is Austin Kevitch’s Age?

On July 5, 1991, Austin Kevitch (age 31) took birth in Pennsylvania, America. His father, Andrew, and mother, Lisa Kevitch, raised him and his siblings, Tristan (brother) and Lindsey (sister), in Philadelphia. There he attended a school named Germantown Academy until his high school graduation.

After schooling, he joined Bucknell University to graduate in Business Management, majoring in computer engineering and minoring in Psychology. During his college years, he was part of the Bucknell Bison football team.

In the second year of college, Kevitch joined an IT company called Scholly. Here his job was that of a product designer. Actually, Scholly is a platform that helps students to get scholarships. In 2013 he received an opportunity to visit the University of Cape Town and study Philosophy.

Austin Kevitch Is the Founder of a Dating Site

After working 2 years as a product designer, Kevitch decided to start his venture. He co-founded a platform named Brighten, an app built to combat bullying. Through this app, one can anonymously give compliments. He worked at Brighten for more than 6 years, and then he moved on to another project.

This time he decided to create a dating app with qualities that he thinks other dating platforms lack. In 2020, he co-founded Lox Club, which got extremely popular. The main reason for the popularity of Lox Club is its exclusivity.

Only persons with high-standard are allowed to be a part of this club. When a person applies for a membership, his profile is reviewed by a committee, which decides if a particular person qualifies or not.

Many people recognize Austin’s dating app as “the dating platform of Jews.” Even stars like Bhad Bhabie and Lil Yachty invested in this app. According to sources, Kevitch currently lives in Los Angeles and has a net worth of USD 5.74 million.
