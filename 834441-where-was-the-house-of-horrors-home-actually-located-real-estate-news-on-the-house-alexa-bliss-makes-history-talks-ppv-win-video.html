Where Was the “House of Horrors” Home Actually Located?

As seen at WWE Payback last night, Bray Wyatt defeated Randy Orton in the first ever House of Horrors match. The match began with pre-recorded footage from a house in Richmond, Missouri off Highway Business 10. According to Movoto.com, the home was a $36,000 house, and you can check out the listing at this link. The bout finished more than 1,800 miles away from the “House of Horrors” set, inside the SAP Center in San Jose, CA.
