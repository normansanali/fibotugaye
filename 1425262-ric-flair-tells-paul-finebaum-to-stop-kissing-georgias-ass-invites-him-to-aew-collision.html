Ric Flair Tells Paul Finebaum To Stop Kissing Georgias Ass, Invites Him To AEW Collision

WWE Hall of Famer and AEW’s newest signee, Ric Flair has been beefing with ESPN Commentator Paul Finebaum. The rivalry began over college football, and it was all thanks to the Michigan Wolverines team.

Finebaum made some comments about the team, which did not sit well with Flair. The 16-time WWE World Heavyweight Champion took to Twitter to send a message to Finebaum.

“The Michigan Wolverines, Denied Your Coach, The Great @CoachJim4UM, You Still Prevailed! HaHa, Big 10, NCAA, And Especially You @finebaum! Quit Being A Hater And Start Being A Believer! Quit Snubbing The Big 10! WOOOOO! @UMichFootball,” Flair tweeted.

In response, Finebaum made some comments about The Nature Boy on the Matt Barrie Show. He claimed that the AEW star was washed up and is looking to get back into the spotlight by making this story a big deal.

Following these comments, The two-time WWE Hall of Famer took to Twitter to retaliate against Finebaum.

“Hey @finebaum, I Spent More Money On Spilt Liquor From One Side Of The World To Another Than You Made Last Year. Stop Kissing Georgia’s Ass! I’ll See You On January 6th In Charlotte- FLAIR COUNTRY (Which You’re Lucky To Live In) For Collision & I’ll Reserve You 2 Front Row Tickets Because God Knows You Can’t Afford To Buy Them! When You Had Me On Your Show, I Was “The Great Ric Flair.” I Still Am & I’m Bigger And Better Than Ever At 74! Let’s Compare W2s Sir! And Also, Your 800k Followers Is Nothing Compared To My 10 Million! FYI- Don’t Ever Joust With The Dirtiest Player In The Game! WOOOOO!” Ric Flair tweeted.
