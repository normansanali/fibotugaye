This Month’s Highlight

The Traitors season 2

Alan Cumming is hosting a whole new cast of reality stars and normies as they try to suss each other out to win a cash prize of up to $250,000 dollars. Set in a Scottish castle, the contestants are all divided into one of two groups (the Traitors and the Faithful) and they try to figure out who is who. Contestants this season include people from Survivor, Love Island, Big Brother, and The Real Housewives franchise. (Streaming January 12.)
