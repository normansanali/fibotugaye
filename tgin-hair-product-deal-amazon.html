Although education about Black history and celebration of Black people should not be confined to just one month, we are taking the occasion to specifically highlight a Black-owned brand in today’s deal: tgin. While Amazon has a Black-Owned Businesses page, check out tgin's store homepage on Amazon to see all of their brand’s products!

A brand born out of adversity, the story behind the creation of Thank God It’s Natural (tgin) is inspiring. During a battle with breast cancer, Chris-Tia Donaldson, CEO of tgin, became aware of the lack of financial resources for women with serious health conditions. She now uses her business to advocate for these women and provide them with the support they need. And that’s a mission we can get behind!

One of many in a line of hair products for curly, kinky, and wavy hair, this styling gel will moisturize your natural hair while holding it in place–all without chemicals. Even better, it’s on a sale for 38% off on Amazon right now!
