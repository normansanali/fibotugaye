EA FC 24 Amazon Prime Gaming Rewards: Release Date, How To Claim

The EA FC 24 Amazon Prime Gaming packs can include great rewards. Here's everything you need to know including the release date and how to claim the rewards.

First Published October 10, 2023, 12:04

The EA FC 24 Amazon Prime Gaming packs look like a great deal for anyone wanting valuable rewards. Judging by the past games, it's very likely that EA will introduce these packs to EA FC 24. Here, we look into the potential release date and how you can claim these packs and rewards in-game.

How To Claim EA FC 24 Amazon Prime Gaming Rewards

The Amazon Prime Gaming rewards will be available to Amazon Prime customers only.  Make sure that the Amazon account you plan to link with your EA Account is the one you will use while you play. Changing your Amazon account later may result in losing access to redeemed rewards on your linked EA account.

How To Link Your Amazon Account To EA FC

Here is how you can link your Amazon account to EA:

  • Go to the Prime Gaming website and select the “Sign In” option located at the top of the page. Ensure that you are signed in with the Amazon account you want to connect to your EA Account.
  • Return to the Prime Gaming homepage after signing in.
  • Find the specific loot you want to claim, and then click the “Claim” button associated with it.
  • It will redirect you to a new page displaying all available, past, and upcoming rewards.
  • Click on “Claim now” for the reward you wish to obtain.
  • A screen will appear, displaying your Amazon Prime account information. Verify that it is indeed the correct account for accessing your content. If it’s not the right one, select “Switch Amazon account” to choose the correct one.
  • Click “Go to Electronic Arts.”
  • You will need to sign in to your Amazon account again after being directed to a new page. Ensure that you use the same account you used in the initial step, and then click “Sign In.”
  • Amazon will ask you to grant Electronic Arts access to your account. Click on “Allow.”
  • Click “Return to Amazon.”
  • Click “Complete claim” to finalize the process.

EA FC 24 Amazon Prime Gaming Rewards and Release Date

EA has not officially released any rewards. However, various leaks hint that the following prizes could be on the way:

  • One 81+ Pick (1 of 3)
  • Four Gold Rare Players
  • Loan Haaland (Eight games)
  • Six Gold Rare Consumables

The Amazon Prime Gaming packs drop every month. In line with previous release dates, we expect this pack to drop somewhere near the middle of October.
