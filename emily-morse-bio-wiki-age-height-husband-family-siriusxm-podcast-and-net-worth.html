Morse is currently is the host of the Sex with Emily radio show on SiriusXM, which airs daily from 8 pm to 10 pm ET on SiriusXM Stars (Ch. 109). She began Sex With Emily back in the year 2005 from her living room in San Francisco and has since become a Doctor of Human Sexuality, author, and multimedia personality.

Emily Morse Podcast

Morse is a sex & relationship expert, author, talk show host & Bravo TV star, Sex With Emily is her podcast about sex, relationships as well as everything in between. As a “sexpert,” she is known for her non-judgmental advice, accessibility, and humor.

Morse has appeared as a guest expert on many radios and television shows, as well as has been featured in The New York Times, The Los Angeles Times as well as San Francisco Chronicle for her expertise in sex and relationships. Morse’s first book Hot Sex: Over 200 Things You Can Try Tonight was released in October the year 2011. She has also acted in, produced, and directed the film, for which she received an award. Morse’s podcast, Sex with Emily, has been in production since the year 2005.

In the year 2012, Morse began a four-year run as a guest co-host on the nationally-syndicated Loveline Radio Show with Dr. Drew Pinsky. She contributes frequently to many major online publications including Glamour, Cosmopolitan, Asks Men, Popsugar, Pattiknows.com, Men’s Health, and Harper’s Bazaar.

IN the year 2014, Morse became the host of the regularly-recurring Sexual Health Expo. She also serves as the sexual health expert for Lifestyles.
