Greg Germann Bio (Wiki)

Who is Greg Germann?

Greg Germann is an American actor, director, and writer. He is popular for his acting skills in Ally McBeal, Ned & Stacey, Grey’s Anatomy, and more.

Moreover, his theatre acts are titled Assassins (1990), Meteor Shower (2016), Boeing-Boeing (2008-09), etc.

Greg Germann- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 26 Feb 1958 and he was born in Houston, Texas, United States. The name of his mother was Marlene Marian Germann (homemaker) and his father was Edward A. Germann (professor).

Greg’s ancestry is Caucasian and regarding his academics, he passed out of high school.

Then he enrolled at the University Of Northern Colorado and graduated in 1978.

Greg Germann- Professional Life, Career

Greg Germann is a popular face of the entertainment world. His first movie was named Streetwalkin‘ of 1985 where he played the role of Creepy.

A few of his other movies are titled The Night We Never Met, The Last Producer, Bigger Than the Sky, Heart of the Beholder, Answers to Nothing, Quitter, Get Hard, Atlas Shrugged: Who Is John Galt?, Get A Job, Billy Boy, and more.

In addition, Greg Germann is noted in TV series like Grey’s Anatomy as Dr. Thomas Koracick, Ned, and Stacey as Eric ‘Rico’ Moyer, Once Upon a Time as Hades, and Sweet Justice as Andy Del Sarto.

For his role of Richard in Ally McBeal (1997-2002), he won Screen Actors Guild Award in 1999 and was nominated for the same award in 2000 plus 2001.

The list of his other notable TV projects are,

  • Tour of Duty as Lt. Beller
  • In Case of Emergency as Sherman Yablonsky
  • House of Lies as Greg Norbert
  • Chasing Life as William Conti
  • One Dollar as Wilson Furlbee
  • Friends from College as Jon Sperling
