About FedEx Transport Company

FedEx Corporation is a United American-based, multinational courier delivery company with its headquarters located in Memphis, Tennessee, in the United States of America. It offers courier and related services to both companies and individuals.

FedEx is the world’s biggest express business, offering fast and reliable delivery services to more than 200 countries, territories, and regions.

It provides expertly-managed services to companies of all sizes, assisting them to protect, ship, and deliver their parcels.

The company has grown from its initial road delivery roots to include rapid and 24-hour air express and ground services. It is the company that came up with The World Of Possibilities slogan.

FedEx Express delivers about 18 million packages a day. It offers reliable overnight delivery across the U.S., Canada, and Latin America and 2-day or three-day services across two or three days between the U.S. and most international destinations.
