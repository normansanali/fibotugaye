What does a flashing green light on a Kidde smoke detector mean?

published on August 19, 2024
Answer AC power is shown by a solid green light on the display. “Battery Only” mode is shown by the green LED flashing every 5 seconds. Hush mode is indicated by a green light flashing every two seconds.
