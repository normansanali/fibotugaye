The Career of Wayne Routledge

published on August 05, 2024
Wayne Routledge, a well-respected soccer player, had an illustrious career that spanned over 20 years, including 10 years with Swansea City. He began his career in London with Crystal Palace as a youth before making senior appearances with the first team. He then moved to Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur for two years but spent part of that time on loan to Portsmouth and Fulham. The surprising alarm-like call of the world's loudest bird

published on August 05, 2024
At 125 decibels, the male white bellbird (Procnias albus) has been named the “World’s Loudest Bird,” or more accurately the loudest bird ever recorded, in a 2019 study [pdf]. Found in the mountain forests of the north-eastern Amazon—”in the Guianas, with small numbers in Venezuela and the Brazilian state of Pará“—the birds’ call sounds metallic, like an electronic warning system alarm.
