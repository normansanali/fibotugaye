The 4 Best KitchenAid Attachments of 2023

Turn your trusty stand mixer into a versatile cooking tool with a few KitchenAid attachments! Find a detailed guide to our favorites below.

My KitchenAid stand mixer has a permanent spot on my countertop. It’s an invaluable tool for making baked goods like cookies, bagels, and pizza dough, but that’s not all. My collection of KitchenAid attachments can turn the mixer into a pasta maker, spiralizer, ice cream maker, and more!

Below, you’ll find my guide to the best KitchenAid attachments. These add-ons can simplify your everyday cooking by doing tasks like slicing, shredding, weighing, and grinding, or they can take the place of an entire standalone appliance, saving space in your cabinets.     Other KitchenAid Attachments to Consider

    I love all the KitchenAid attachments that I own. However, there are still a few that I have yet to add to my collection and others that don’t fit my cooking needs but would be great for someone else! These popular and highly rated attachments are worth checking out as well:

    Best for Bread Baking: KitchenAid Sifter + Scale Attachment

    I’ve seen the KitchenAid Sifter + Scale attachment in person and wish I had it when I was baking lots of bread at home a few years ago. It would have come in super handy for measuring ingredients for the dough. I’d recommend it for avid bakers to simplify measuring flour and other dry ingredients. The KitchenAid Sifter + Scale Attachment is available at Amazon for $99.99.

    Best for Shredding: KitchenAid Fresh Prep Slicer/Shredder Attachment

    Love cheese but don’t want to grate it manually? The KitchenAid Fresh Prep Slicer/Shredder Attachment would be a great attachment for you, especially if you don’t own a food processor. I usually grate by hand, but this attachment, which comes with three blades for shredding cheese and vegetables, would be a major timesaver if you’re cooking large quantities for meal prep or entertaining. It’s also quite affordable compared to other KitchenAid attachments. The KitchenAid Fresh Prep Slicer/Shredder Attachment is available at Amazon for $44.95.

    Best for Whole Grain Lovers: KitchenAid All Metal Grain Mill Attachment

    This KitchenAid attachment is for all the whole grain bakers out there. The KitchenAid All Metal Grain Mill grinds wheat, oats, corn, rice, and more into great homemade flours. You can adjust the texture to add some extra flair to baked goods and breads. Just note it can be a little loud. The KitchenAid All Metal Grain Mill Attachment is available at Amazon for $114.95.

    Best for Summertime: KitchenAid Shave Ice Attachment

    Hello, summer! The KitchenAid Shave Ice Attachment is new to the KitchenAid lineup, and it’s on my list to try ASAP. You can use it to make shaved ice for dessert or add crushed ice to drinks, and you can add syrups, coffee, and more directly into the attachment. It would be a great addition to any backyard party! The KitchenAid Shave Ice Attachment is available at Amazon for $74.95.
