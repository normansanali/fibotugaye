Jason Belmonte Biography

Jason Belmonte is best known as Professional Bowler who has an estimated Net Worth of $1 Million. Champion ten-pin bowler won several PBA Tour events in the U.S., and took part international competitions. He was born on July 29,1983, Australia. Participating at the World Tenpin Masters in England, he had the first ever 300 game in the history of the event. His father ran a local bowling alley New South Wales, where Jason began rolling the ball as early as 18 months of age. He used two hands, a style he continued to use growing up. He defeated Sean Rash in the finals to clinch the 2010 Korea Cup title. He married Kimberly Shapter in 2007. His zodiac sign is Leo.
