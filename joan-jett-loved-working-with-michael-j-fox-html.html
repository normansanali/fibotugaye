Joan Jett Loved Working With Michael J. Fox

Rock legend Joan Jett stopped by the Sundance Vulture Spot with her longtime collaborator Kenny Laguna to chat about their documentary Bad Reputation, which chronicles her incredible 40-year music career. She also reminisced about what it was like to act in the 1987 film Light of Day with actor Michael J. Fox.
