GoFundME: Austin Trebesch Dies In Farm Implement Accident

A 27-year-old Sleepy Eye man died in a work-related farm implement accident reported at 8:43 a.m. Tuesday.

Austin Brett Trebesch was treated by first responders at the scene, 22024 State Highway 4, Home Township. He was transported to Sleepy Eye Medical Center where he was pronounced dead, according to the Brown County Sheriff’s Office. No further accident details were available.

The Brown County Sheriff’s Office was assisted by Sleepy Eye Police, Sleepy Eye Ambulance and North Memorial Air Care.

The accident remains under investigation with the Brown County Sheriff’s Office and Minnesota Department of Labor and Industry.
