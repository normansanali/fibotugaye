Kanye Shows Off Buff Biceps on Outing With Kim Kardashian

New workout plan? On Monday, March, 28, Kanye West showed off his ripped arms while stepping out with wife Kim Kardashian in Los Angeles.

The parents of North, 2, and Saint, 3 months, were spotted leaving a taping of Khloé Kardashian’s talk show Kocktails with Khloé, for which the “Famous” rapper, 38, put his bulging biceps on display in a loose-fitting tank top.

Looking at his massive muscles, it appears as though Yeezus has been working hard on his upper body strength. He raps on his new The Life of Pablo track “30 Hours,” “I hit the gym, all chest, no legs.” In another song, “Highlights,” from his latest LP, West makes it clear that fitness has been at the forefront of his mind when he spits the line: “I need every bad b–ch up in Equinox.”

While Kardashian, 35, didn’t show as much skin as her hubby, she stunned nonetheless in a form-fitting frock teamed with a long black coat, Hermès bag and Stuart Weitzman sandals.
