In his sophomore year, Skylar Thompson had been down with minor injuries, including a broken rib and a twisted ankle, and in 2020, Thompson was ruled out-for-season after sustaining a serious upper-body injury against Texas Tech that required “immediate surgery”.

Teresa Thompson’s death: What happened to Skylar Thompson’s mother?

Skylar John Thompson (born June 4, 1997) is an American football quarterback for the Miami Dolphins of the National Football League (NFL).

When Skylar was just six years old, his family especially his mother was hit by a tragedy. His mother Teresa was diagnosed with stage-four breast cancer and the doctors told his family that chemotherapy would not help her in her recovery.

Teresa and her husband, Brad had traveled across the USA in search of the best treatment for her. She received chemotherapy at the Cancer Treatment Center of America, Tulsa, Oklahoma.

Unfortunately, on May 11, 2004, Teresa Lynn Thompson passed away, only two days after Mother’s Day.
