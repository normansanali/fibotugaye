Meet 7 Little Johnstons Star Dacey Triplett! Her Bio, Age, Dating, Parents

TLC’s 7 Little Johnstons follows the lives of The Johnstons, a family of seven suffering from dwarfism. Elizabeth Renee Johnstone, aka Liz Johnston, is the second biological child of her parents, Trent and Amber Johnston.

Lately, as we can see in the show, Liz’s social life has had a huge boom as she spends a lot of time with her besties, Carol and Dacey, and her boyfriend, Brice. Since Liz regularly sees her friend Dacey, it’s only natural that the viewers of the show want to know more about her.

In this wiki article, we will talk about Dacey Triplett’s appearances on 7 Little Johnstons, her friendship with Liz, her job, and more.

Meet Liz’s Friend Dacey Triplett On 7 Little Johnstons

Liz’s friend Dacey Triplett first made her appearance in 7 Little Johnstons in January 2021 and as of this writing, she has appeared in quite a few episodes already.

Dacey Danielle Triplett made her debut in the TLC show alongside Liz’s other friend Carol. She had joined Liz, her boyfriend Brice, and Carol on their fishing trip. In her debut episode, she had some intense interrogation with Liz’s boyfriend Brice. Dacey grilled Brice by asking him how tall he is, how many children he wants, whether he has been arrested before–to which he said no–and if he is a daredevil.

As viewers have seen in the show, it’s quite evident that Liz considers Dacey one of her closest friends, alongside her boyfriend Brice and another bestie Carol. Liz occasionally features Dacey in her Instagram feed, whenever she is taking her to meet Brice or while they are going for a girls’ weekend.

Some of Liz and Dacey’s recent adventures include getting dressed up for their friend Ariel Barker’s wedding, hiking a hill, and rowing on a lake.

Dacey Triplett Age

Born in 2002, Dacey’s age was 19 as of this writing in March 2022.

Dacey Triplett Job

Dacey Tripplett completed her high school at West Wilkes High School in Georgia. While she was at West Wilkes High School, she was a part of baseball operations there. During her time there, she was a team manager and statistician for the Blackhawk baseball program.

As a manager of West Wilkes’ baseball team, they won three conference championships in a row and made it to the playoffs all three of those years. She was also a part of the first women’s basketball conference championship in West Wilkes history.

Dacey was also a member of the executive council on the 2019-2020 Hawks student basketball team. Cross of Cancer reported that Dacey won $1000 as one of the recipients of The Kevin Wayne Triplett Memorial Scholarship.

Talking to Cross of Cancer, she told, “I have been blessed with an amazing life, but I have had to face some hardships. The older I get the more I realize being different is not as bad as people make it out to be. I LOVE being a little person. I love the person God created me to be.”

Outside of sports, Dacey’s biggest achievement was winning Ms. West High. She was named Ms. West High while she was in her senior year at West Wilkes.

Currently, Dacey is a student at Appalachian State University studying nutrition. Set to graduate in 2024, Dacey aims to become a dietician.

Is Dacey Triplett Dating Anyone?

It’s unlikely that Dacey Triplett was dating anyone in 2022. We suspect that Liz’s bestie was still single as of this writing.

That being said, Dacey did write something for her future love in the social platform VSCO. The message reads: ‘Won’t the final result be beautiful? I can’t wait to finally hold your hand… I can’t wait to simply love you… I’m forever praying for you.”

Who Are Dacey Triplett’s Parents?

Dacey Triplett’s parents are Christy Church Triplett (mother) and Daniel Triplett (father). Dacey’s parents got married in the year 1995, according to her mom Christy’s Facebook.

Dacey has two siblings, a brother, and a sister. They are Noah and Kacey Triplett.

Reportedly, Dacey’s brother Noah completed his four-month internship at Yellowstone National Park and nearby areas of Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho and had the best time of his life. This 4-month internship enabled West Wilkes High School’s graduate to get into Appalachian State University in Boone and study recreation management.

Dacey Triplett Height

Like her best friend Liz, Dacey also lives with dwarfism. Her height was 4 feet 1 inch as of March 2022.
