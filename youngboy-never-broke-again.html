YoungBoy Never Broke Again

Kentrell DeSean Gaulden, known professionally as YoungBoy Never Broke Again, also known as NBA YoungBoy or simply YoungBoy, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. NBA Youngboy was born on October 20, 1999. He’s a member of the “Never Broke Again Crew” hence the origin of his name. NBA YoungBoy has been noted for his melodic vocals and unique signature aggressive lyrics with high energy.
