Who does Mike Taibbi work for? Is Mike Taibbi still alive?

Mike Taibbi (born c. 1949) is an American television journalist best known for his work at NBC News.

Who does Mike Taibbi work for?

Mike Taibbi worked for NBC News, and Taibbi also worked at CBS News. He is the recipient of an Emmy Award and a four-time recipient of the prestigious Edward R. Murrow Award.

Early in his career, Taibbi worked in local television news in Boston and New York City. Subsequently, he worked at both ABC News and CBS News before joining NBC News, in 1997, to work on Dateline NBC. In the early 2000s, Taibbi reported on the Iraq and Afghanistan Wars for NBC News.

Is Mike Taibbi still alive?

Yes, He retired in 2014, having covered, among other events, the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan. During his career, Taibbi also worked at CBS News. He is the recipient of an Emmy Award and a four-time recipient of the prestigious Edward R. Murrow Award. He is currently based in New York.
