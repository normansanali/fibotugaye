Faith Kelly - Age, Family, Bio

English TikTok star famous for posting comedy, lip-sync, and fitness videos. She often makes stitches and uses audio from other content creators. Her faithlouisak account has gained 750,000 followers and 34 million likes. 

Before Fame

She began posting to TikTok on April 9, 2020. 

Trivia

She posts modeling photos on her faithlouisak account, which has amassed 410,000 followers. One of her most popular TikToks announces that she is pregnant. It has earned over 3 million views. She has seen many of her videos earn view counts in the multi-millions. 

Family Life

She posted a photo of her grandparents on June 7, 2020. She has dated fellow social media star Ethan Payne. On April 15th 2022, the couple announced that they were expecting their first child together, a daughter. 

Associated With

She posted a video featuring music by Lil Nas X.
